I'm a 1st year PhD Student in Statistics at Stanford University. Before that, I was a Scientific Associate at [D.E. Shaw Research](https://www.deshawresearch.com) ("DESRES"). And even before that, I was an undergraduate at [Stanford University](https://www.stanford.edu). In 2017, I graduated with a B.S. in Mathematical and Computational Science and an M.S. in Statistics. While there, I was lucky enough to be advised by [Brad Efron](http://statweb.stanford.edu/~ckirby/brad/) and worked on projects involving the oral microbiome and card shuffling with [Susan Holmes](http://statweb.stanford.edu/~susan/susan_person.html) and [Persi Diaconis](https://statweb.stanford.edu/~cgates/PERSI/), respectively.
