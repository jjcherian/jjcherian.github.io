I'm a Scientific Associate at [D.E. Shaw Research](https://www.deshawresearch.com) ("DESRES"). Since 2017, I've been working on improving force fields for biomolecular simulation. The methods I have worked on include:
* An application of the fused LASSO to atom type selection for small-molecule force fields
* An asynchronous, stochastic second-order optimization method for nonlinear least-squares problems
* An objective and method for efficient hyperparameter optimization in order to achieve improved generalization on unseen validation data
